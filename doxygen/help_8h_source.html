33THROTTLE: Sets the speed and direction of a locomotive.
34
35Syntax: <THROTTLE cab="<CAB>" speed="<SPEED>" direction="<DIR>" steps="<STEPS>">
36 <CAB> - The locomotive's DCC address.
37 <SPEED> - Speed as a percentage (0-100).
38 <DIR> - Direction: 1 for FORWARD, 0 for REVERSE.

40
41Legacy Syntax: <t <CAB> <SPEED> <DIR>>

43
44Examples:
45 <THROTTLE cab="3" speed="50" direction="1"> - Sets loco 3 to 50% speed, forward.
